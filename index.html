Yan Shi

I am a Ph.D. student in the StarLab at the University of Washington, advised by Prof. Yinhai Wang. My research interests lie in computer vision, machine learning, and vision-language models (VLMs), with a particular focus on spatio-temporal reasoning, multimodal perception, and intelligent transportation systems. My work aims to advance interpretable and reliable AI for transportation safety and mobility.

Previously, I received my M.S. degree in Big Data from Tsinghua University in 2023, advised by Prof. Kai Zhang, and my B.S. degree in Computer Science from Shandong University in 2020.

I am open to collaboration opportunities at the intersection of vision, language, and transportation AI. Please feel free to reach out if you are interested in discussing research ideas.
